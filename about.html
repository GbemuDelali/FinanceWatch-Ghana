About Finance Watch Ghana

Accountability. Integrity. Financial Justice.

Our Mission

To advocate for a fair, transparent, and inclusive financial system in Ghana that serves the needs of all citizens through evidence-based research, policy engagement, and public empowerment.

Our Vision

To become Ghana's leading civic watchdog in financial governance, advancing economic justice and institutional accountability through informed citizen participation and regulatory reform.
