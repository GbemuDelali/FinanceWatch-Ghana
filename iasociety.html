About the IAS Bootcamp

The Investment Analysts Society (IAS) Bootcamp is a flagship professional development initiative by Finance Watch Ghana aimed at training and equipping the next generation of financial analysts, policy advisors, and economic researchers.

The program combines technical finance, equity research, ethics, ESG, and policy advocacy in a structured learning journey.   • Data Analytics for Investment
  • Ghana Capital Market Regulations
  • Research & Publication Standards
